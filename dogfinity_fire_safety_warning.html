Dogfinity by Mimicry

Regulatory Compliance and Safety Information


WARNINGS

Failure to follow warnings and instructions may cause fire or injury


  • Do not block vents holes.

  • Place device on a stable heat resistant surface.

  • Place device at least 6 inches from the wall.

  • 1ft of empty space above the device should be reserved for heat management.

  • This product should be operated in a well ventilated environment away from flammable objects. The device should not be covered.

  • A fire may occur if the product is covered or touching flammable materials such as curtains, draperies, walls and the like when in operation. Do not operate under wall cabinets.

  • Do not use with extension cords.


  • Device should not run for more than 6 continuous hours. Do NOT leave device unattended while in use. Unplug when not in use.

  • After running, do not turn back on until all parts return to room temperature.

  • Dispose of device if cables become frayed.


INSTRUCTIONS FOR SAFE USE

  • Do not expose the device to water, moisture or place near a flammable surface whilst in operation

  • Do not expose the device to heat from any source; the device is designed for reliable operation at normal ambient room temperatures.

  • Avoid Handling the device while it is powered.

  • To protect against electrical shock, do not immerse product or attached cords in water or other liquids.

  • Do not use outdoors.


Warning and instructions are subject to change without prior notice. Please read the latest instructions at www.mimicry.co/dogfinity_fire_safety_warning
