Infinite Animation (IA) Artwork is a novel type of art which joins the artist’s vision and AI to continuously generate brand new scenes - forever. This unique setup ensures that each time a piece is viewed, something new is happening - for the first time - right before the viewers eyes.

Infinite Animation relies on AI to aid in the creation of the scenes unfolding before the viewer. But AI is just a tool, the artist is always in control and is the key to making the infinite set of new scenes interesting to the viewer.

Like many other pieces of generated art, Infinite Animation is the fusion of an artist’s vision and AI algorithms. The scenes, designs, shapes, algorithms, must all be meticulously selected by the artist. The artist considers not only what the art looks like today but how it could evolve into the future.

Infinite Animation is set apart from previous generated art in its flexibility of the algorithms to showcase various art styles - ones that represent the artist themselves. No longer is generated art limited to floating polygons and moving lines - each element of the overall piece is a masterpiece of animated art itself, joined with a meticulously written algorithm so that it has it’s own unique life - mirroring the dynamic and unique nature of life itself.
