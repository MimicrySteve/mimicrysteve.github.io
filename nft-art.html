INFINITE ANIMATION AND NFTS
Infinite Animation (IA) artwork is a unique and expressive art form on it’s own. But it becomes supercharged when you link it with blockchain technology. In fact, the concept of infinite animation was initially conceived with blockchain in mind. The reason: this perpetual nature of infinite animation solves art NFTs biggest problems.
- PERPETUAL
What distinguishes infinite animation artwork from other art forms is that it is always updating. It’s value proposition as a medium is in it’s ability to capture the attention of the viewer over a long period of time. Different messages can be conveyed. And more time is spent studying/understanding/observing every single piece. Artists must think about how they want each piece to evolve. And viewers have a reason to keep coming back: to learn the artist’s full message. The best persistent artist know how to make that process last forever. Owning a piece of infinite animation artwork means owning something that is forever a source of enjoyment and fascination. Click here to read more about the perpetual quality of Infinite Animation Artwork.
- UNCOPYABLE
IA is philosophically and practically uncopyable. The important visual scenes of any single piece are the scenes that have not yet been created. A thief can’t record an unaired video. They can’t copy/paste a JPEG that doesn’t yet exist. Therefore, there is no way to rip off an artists important work. A thief can only record the past and therefore can never ruin the value proposition of any piece’s future. Some additional rights are bestowed to owners - check the WIKI for more information or click here to learn more about the uncopyable quality of Infinite Animation Artwork.
- SCARCE
Each piece of infinite animation artwork requires a significant amount of effort from the artist. 10,000 pieces can’t be created in an instant. An artist can make maybe a couple thousand pieces in a lifetime. Because of this, the artist’s name, reputation, and future livelihood is tethered to the success of each piece. It’s like physical art. And therefore artists are incentivized to create an individual style and ensure that their work remains unique. Click here to learn more about the scarce quality of Infinite Animation Artwork.
- VALUABLE
A piece of artwork that continues to provide more and more content into perpetuity is an asset who’s value is easily understood. In fact, infinite animation artwork is capable of generating offshoot NFT’s as well. The value of each piece comes from these two sources.
Only the owner has the right to view the future of a piece. They can choose to keep it a secret or share it wildly, host a viewing party and charge admissions. The right is theirs alone. And they own the right to showcase the future when and if they choose
Generated offshoot NFTs can be sold separately. Either granting rights to a specific image or scene to a buyer, or just as a separate purchasable JPEG/GIF/Video
These two sources of assets make up a cash flow for owning a piece. And future cash flow means a present value.
The first easy to understand investment case for NFTs. Click here to learn more about the valuable quality of Infinite Animation Artwork.
PERPETUAL. UNCOPYABLE. SCARCE. VALUABLE.
The same question get thrown around over and over with NFTs:
The issue is clearly understood. What is the point of “owning” an art NFT when you don’t receive any significant legal rights. And even worse: the product you “buy” can easily be copy/pasted by anybody. The persistence of infinite animation 100% solves these very issues - making ownership special, desirable, and exclusive. Furthermore, persistence guarantees that nobody is able to copy/paste artists’ NFTs.
