Parad is a strong believer in crypto's ability to bring about positive change in the world - only supporting crypto endeavors with morally strong business models, open financials and strong disclosures.

Previously, Parad was a portfolio manager at one of largest commodities focused hedge funds worldwide. While originally trained in the energy markets, Parad was one of the first crypto analysts on wall street focused on covering crypto starting in 2015.

Parad is a graduate of the University of Pennsylvania holding three degrees - undergraduate studies in Wharton and graduate studies in mathematics and computer science focusing on machine learning.
Steven Parad - Founder
The idea for Mimicry came about after years of discussions revolving around the failures of copyright, the interaction of computer science and artwork, the possibilities of new crypto infrastructure, and the resurgence of Austrian economics. However implementation was finally evangelized during the winter of 2021 when low effort blockchain artwork become popular - artwork which sullied the potential of this technology to completely revolutionize how we interact with artwork.

Mimicry is the corporation which produces and maintains the software for creating Infinite Animations and associated NFTs. As such mimicry needs a for-profit business model. However Mimicry's philosophy is one of open discourse and clarity stating what services are being provided and how each of them requires effort from Mimicry to maintain. To learn more about Mimicry's business model, visit the page on services provided.

Mimicry strongly considers moving any and all revenue streams from corporation to a token model - where token holders receive the profits - if this can be done in a non-preditory, non-cash grab method. This is a developing endeavor.
